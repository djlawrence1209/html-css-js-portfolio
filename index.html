About Me

How it Started

It all started back in high school when I took a web development course. I was hooked by the idea of building anything I could imagine, just by telling a computer what to do in its own language. That early exposure sparked a deep curiosity about how software works and how it can be used to solve real-world problems.


I went on to study Computer Science at Florida International University, where I pursued my B.A. Throughout my undergraduate journey, I discovered just how fascinating the theory behind computer science can be, from algorithms to systems design. I collaborated with peers on full-stack projects, competed in hackathons, and joined workshops that challenged me to build impactful applications under real constraints. Working in teams taught me not only how to code, but how to lead, listen, and grow.

Where we are going

Recently, I was accepted into FIU's Master's in Data Science and AI, marking the next chapter in my academic journey. This transition reflects my passion for exploring machine learning, statistics, and intelligent systems at a deeper level. I'm also beginning a research position with Algoverse as a Machine Learning Researcher , where I'll contribute to academic work focused on algorithmic innovation.


With every step, my goal remains the same: to use my skills to build meaningful technology that makes the world better. I'm especially excited to continue this journey with the support of my AI assistant, Sky, as I explore the frontier of intelligent, data-driven systems.
